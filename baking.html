🍰 Baking Learning Platform

A gamified digital platform that helps users learn baking through interactive recipes, quizzes, and progression-based content.

Overview

Problem

Beginner bakers often struggle to stay engaged while learning recipes, leading to low retention and incomplete learning experiences.

Goal

Design a structured and engaging learning experience that combines education, interaction, and progression.

Role

UX Design
UI Design
Product Concept Development

System Design

The platform is designed as a progression-based system where users move through recipes, complete quizzes, and unlock new content. This creates a structured learning journey rather than isolated recipe browsing.

Key Features

Gamified recipe progression
Quiz-based unlocking system
Integrated checkout flow
Social activity feed
Messaging & support

User Flow

Users begin by selecting a recipe, follow step-by-step instructions, and complete a quiz to reinforce learning. Successful completion unlocks new recipes and features, encouraging continued engagement.

Challenges

A key challenge was designing a system that balances learning and engagement without overwhelming users. Structuring the flow in a way that feels intuitive while still encouraging progression required careful planning.

Final Outcome

Reflection

This project helped me understand how to design multi-feature product systems that combine learning, engagement, and commerce. In future iterations, I would refine the onboarding experience and further personalize the learning journey.
